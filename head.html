The Nervous System

Sensitive
It feels stimulus both from INSIDE (i.e. blood pressure) and OUTSIDE the body (i. e. a drop of water in your hand).
Integrative
The sensitive information is analyzed and some responses are developed to face the stimulus (reactions and behaviour).
Motor
The body responses to that stimulus by reacting, both voluntarily as well as non-intentionally. (i.e. gastric secretions, breathing faster, moving a hand, etc.).
