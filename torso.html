The Torso

Pelvic Functions

Locomotion

Energy efficiency while moving:

•Maximizing muscular power

•Offering better lever action between upper and lower body

•Lowering load weight on lower body

Thermoregulation

Maintaining optimal temperature:

•Affected by width and depth of pelvis

•Determination of body proportions → body area-to-mass ratio

•Heat loss control (more surface → more heat loss, and vice versa)

Parturition

Delivery of a healthy infant will not harm the mother:

•Affected by width and depth of pelvis

•Strong muscular structure
