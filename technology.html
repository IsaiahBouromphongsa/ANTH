• Originally for researchers, now used for communication, commerce, entertainment, and more

• World Wide Web provides access to vast information and services

Fridge

• Appliance for storing food and drinks at low temperatures

• Works by removing heat using a refrigerant gas cycle and compressor

• Available in various sizes and designs for home use

• Helps to preserve food and reduce waste.

Plow

• Agricultural tool for tilling soil and planting crops

• Used for thousands of years, with early versions made from wood or bones

• Iron plow in the Middle Ages improved efficiency and productivity

• Key to rise of civilization, allowing large-scale crop cultivation and settled societies.
